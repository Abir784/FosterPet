Manage Pets

Maintain the pet database by adding new animals, updating profiles, or removing inactive listings. Includes filters for status (available, adopted, pending) and breed.

  • Add or edit pet profiles
  • Update photos and medical info
  • Track foster/adoption status

Adoption & Foster Applications

Quickly review incoming adoption and foster applications. Sort by urgency or applicant background, and approve or reject with comments.

  • View applicant details
  • Assign reviewers
  • Auto-send email notifications

Users

Oversee user accounts including adopters, pet owners, and shelter partners. Monitor account activity and access control levels.

  • Ban/report suspicious users
  • Grant admin/mod access
  • Analyze user trends

Messages

Facilitate safe and moderated communication between shelters and adopters. Flag inappropriate content and archive old conversations.

  • Search message history
  • Filter by user or date
  • Flag for moderation

Donations

Track donation history, generate monthly reports, and highlight top donors. Integration with payment gateways supported.

  • Monitor real-time donations
  • Export reports to CSV
  • Manage donor communications
